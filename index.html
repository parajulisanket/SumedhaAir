About Us

Experience the Sky,
Redefined

Sumedha Air is a premier helicopter company based in Nepal, specializing in safe, efficient, and high-altitude operations. With the trusted Airbus H125 in our fleet—known for its unmatched performance in mountainous terrain. We provide access to some of the most remote and breathtaking destinations in Nepal.

We are committed to excellence in safety, hospitality, and reliability, catering to both corporate and leisure travelers, as well as emergency and logistical operations.
