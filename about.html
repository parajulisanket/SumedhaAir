About Us

Soaring Beyond Expectations.
Your Journey, Our Passion.

Sumedha Air is Nepal’s premier helicopter tour company, based in Kathmandu. We offer luxury heli-tour adventures across Nepal’s most stunning landscapes, combining the highest safety standards with personalized service and expert pilots.

At Sumedha Air, our core values guide everything we do. Safety is our highest priority—every flight operates under the strictest safety protocols, ensuring peace of mind for our guests. We uphold Integrity by maintaining transparency, honesty, and ethical practices in all our operations. Driven by a passion for Excellence, we are committed to delivering outstanding service and unforgettable experiences on every journey.

  • Expand services and set new standards in aviation.
  • Fully certified for safety and operational excellence.
  • Committed to sustainability and community responsibility.
