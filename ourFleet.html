Our Fleet

Airbus H125 – Performance & Safety at Altitude

At Sumedha Air, we fly with purpose—and with the best tools for the job. Our fleet is anchored by the world-renowned Airbus H125, a helicopter trusted globally for its high-altitude performance, safety, and adaptability. It’s the aircraft of choice for missions that demand both power and finesse.

Modern, versatile & maintained for ultimate safety and comfort.

Why the Airbus H125?

The Airbus H125 is built for Nepal’s extreme conditions. Its lightweight frame and powerful engine make it uniquely capable of flying at high elevations, where other helicopters simply can’t perform.

  • High-Altitude Mastery: Operates above 23,000 ft (7,000+ m)
  • Passenger Capacity: Up to 5 passengers + pilot
  • Safran Arriel 2D engine for power and reliability
  • Dual hydraulics, crash-resistant fuel tanks, advanced avionics
  • Multi-mission: rescue, tourism, utility, filming, cargo
  • Low noise & fuel efficient with reduced acoustic footprint

Ideal For

  • VIP & Executive Travel
  • Aerial Sightseeing Tours
  • Emergency Medical Evacuation (MEDEVAC)
  • High-Altitude Cargo Transport
  • Rescue & Disaster Response
  • Aerial Photography & Film Shoots
